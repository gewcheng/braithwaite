Why we exist Services About Connect

Cultural institutions navigate a world where credibility and visibility determine impact. The challenge isn’t awareness. It’s strategic fluency to connect institutional value to the people who matter.

Our approach is grounded in fifteen years of editorial and consulting experience spanning arts organizations, foundations, and public institutions. We help clients articulate their value, build visibility, and deepen impact.

We focus on mission-level alignment, long-term relationship cultivation, and narrative clarity—so that institutional story and strategy work in tandem.

We offer strategic collaboration without agency overhead.

Strategic Communications

We focus institutional narratives, launch programs, and position institutions within critical discourse. Messaging, media strategy, and thought leadership are tailored to each client’s goals and audience.

Programming & Partnership Development

We build sustainable relationships across contexts: municipal cultural departments, international exchange programs, corporate engagement, and institutional collaborations. From scoping to execution, we align partners and outcomes.

Individual Advising

We counsel artists and cultural leaders at career inflection points. The work addresses positioning, infrastructure, and advancement: creating clarity that transforms into momentum, and strategy that sustains growth.

Hunter Braithwaite advises cultural institutions, foundations, and individual practitioners. He holds an MFA from New York University.

Hunter serves on the Board of Directors of Storefront for Art and Architecture and is a member of Professional Advisors to the International Art Market.
